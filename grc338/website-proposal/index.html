Introduction

Laguna Coffee Co.

Lagune Coffee Co. is a small family owned coffee shop located in Laguna Beach, Califonia. Laguna Coffee Co. loves everything coffe and offers everything from classic lattes to cold brews. The shop has an amazing view of the beach and is a beautiful study space for Laguna Beach locals to enjoy a cup of cofee and more.

Target audience

The target audience is shop lovers or people who love to spend time at cafes where cheaper priced coffee is available with an inviting and home-like atmosphere. They are very tech savvy and want to be able to easily order coffee and read about new releases on the website.

The customers' goal is to get a quality cup of coffee and find a good spot to sit and work on emails, homework, or just come in to read a good book. On this website they will mostly use it to order coffee so it is ready when they arrive or purchase coffee beans, mugs, and other merchandise.
